Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new group:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to the Groups page
  3. Click the "Create New Group" button
  4. Fill in the group name and description
  5. Choose a group image or upload your own
  6. Click "Create Group" to finish

To join a group:
  1. Navigate to the group's page
  2. Click the "Join Group" button
  3. Once joined, you can participate in discussions and create posts
Note: You must be logged in to join groups.

To create a post:
  1. Join the group if you haven't already
  2. Go to the group's page
  3. Find the "Create Post" form
  4. Enter your post title and content
  5. Optionally add an image
  6. Click "Create Post" to publish

To update your profile:
  1. Click on your username or profile picture
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Update your information (location, profile picture)
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update

Privacy information:
  • Your email address is never shared publicly
  • Only your username and location are visible to other users
  • Group posts are visible to all group members
  • You can leave a group at any time
